FAQ

Direct answers to the questions Texas owners ask.

What is a QuickBooks ProAdvisor, and what does WestgateFS handle?

A QuickBooks ProAdvisor is a bookkeeping or accounting professional credentialed by Intuit on QuickBooks Online and Desktop. WestgateFS is an independent ProAdvisor practice handling file review, cleanup, ongoing Online and Desktop management, QBD-to-QBO migration, and monthly bookkeeping for small businesses, nonprofits, and churches across Texas. We are not Intuit support, and we are not a CPA firm.

How is WestgateFS different from QuickBooks Live?

QuickBooks Live is Intuit’s productized bookkeeping service, staffed by rotating bookkeepers from a queue. WestgateFS is an independent ProAdvisor practice where the same senior operators stay on your file every month, with full responsibility for your books and direct access to senior judgment. Different model, different work, different outcomes. See the full comparison.

Do you provide technical support?

No. We’re not Intuit technical support. If your software has a license issue, a sync error, a payroll-tax filing question, or any other product-level concern, contact Intuit directly. WestgateFS handles the bookkeeping inside the software — the judgment about your numbers, not the platform itself.

Should I be on QuickBooks Online or Desktop?

For most Texas small businesses today, Online is the right answer — cloud-native, modern app integrations, fully remote-capable. For some construction, manufacturing, and inventory-heavy operators, Desktop still wins on job costing and inventory features. We’ll tell you honestly which one fits your business after reviewing your file. QuickBooks Online · QuickBooks Desktop.

How long does a QuickBooks cleanup take?

Most cleanups take between four and ten weeks, depending on how many months are out of order, how many accounts need reconciliation, how complex the chart of accounts is, and how clean the source documents are. We give a written timeline after a free diagnostic review. More on cleanup.

How does QBD-to-QBO migration work?

We diagnose the source Desktop file first, map the chart of accounts to Online, validate historicals, run the migration in a controlled window, and reconcile every account afterward. Lists, transactions, and balances are all verified before we sign off. No one-click gambles. More on migration.

How is ongoing management priced?

Monthly management is priced like monthly bookkeeping — based on transaction volume, the number of bank and credit card accounts, the complexity of your industry, and the reporting cadence you need. Cleanup and migration engagements are scoped after a written diagnostic. We never quote until we’ve reviewed a recent month of activity. See our pricing approach.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use accountant access — never shared admin logins. Documents move through an encrypted portal. Every system uses multi-factor authentication and least-privilege controls. Read our data security practices.
