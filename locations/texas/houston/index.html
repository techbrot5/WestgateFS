Industries we serve in Houston

We understand Houston’s business landscape.

Houston has one of the most diverse small-business economies in the United States. WestgateFS serves the industries that drive it — each with its own bookkeeping demands, transaction patterns, and compliance context.

Restaurants & Hospitality

Houston has one of the largest restaurant markets in the US. High transaction volume, tip reconciliation, cost-of-goods tracking, multi-location P&L, and tight margin management. We have worked with Houston restaurant groups of every size.

Energy & Oil Field Services

Houston is the energy capital of the US. Contractors, service companies, and equipment operations in the energy sector need clean operational accounting. We manage QuickBooks for energy-sector clients with complex billing cycles and project tracking.

Construction & Specialty Contractors

Greater Houston is one of the largest construction markets in Texas. Job costing, subcontractor tracking, progress billing, retainage, and lien waiver management require bookkeeping that understands how construction revenue and expenses actually flow.

Healthcare & Medical Practices

Independent practices and specialty groups across Greater Houston. Insurance payment reconciliation, revenue cycle context, multiple entity management, and HIPAA-aware process. We do not provide billing services — we manage the bookkeeping layer that sits behind your billing system.

Professional Services

Law firms, consulting practices, engineering firms, and business services companies in Houston. Revenue recognition, retainer management, unbilled work-in-progress, and accounts receivable for service businesses with complex billing arrangements.

Nonprofits & Churches

Greater Houston has a large and active nonprofit sector. Fund accounting, grant tracking, restricted and unrestricted fund separation, and board-ready financial reporting for Houston nonprofits and churches operating on QuickBooks.

FAQ — Houston

Questions about WestgateFS in Houston.

Do you have an office in Houston I can visit?

No. WestgateFS is based in Conroe, Texas — approximately 35 miles north of downtown Houston via I-45. We serve Houston businesses entirely remotely through QuickBooks Online and Desktop. No site visits are needed at any stage of an engagement. Your books are in QuickBooks; that’s where we work. Discovery calls, onboarding, and monthly close are all handled remotely — the same way Houston businesses typically work with their CPAs.

How does remote bookkeeping work for Houston businesses?

You grant WestgateFS accountant-level access to your QuickBooks Online or Desktop file. We handle all transaction recording, categorization, reconciliation, monthly close, and reporting from our Conroe office. You see the updated file in real time. We share monthly reports on your schedule. At year-end, we package the file for your CPA or coordinate with them directly. Nothing in the process requires us to be physically in Houston.

Do you work with Houston-area CPAs?

Yes. We coordinate with your CPA directly and can provide them accountant access to your QuickBooks file at any time. We are not a CPA firm — we do not file tax returns or provide tax advice. We handle the bookkeeping layer: clean, reconciled records your Houston CPA can file from. Many Houston-area CPAs prefer receiving an already-reconciled file from a dedicated bookkeeper rather than spending the first days of tax season untangling the year’s records.
