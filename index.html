FAQ

Direct answers to the questions Texas owners actually ask.

What does Westgate Financial Services do?

Westgate Financial Services (WFS / WestgateFS) is a Texas bookkeeping practice based at 15834 Berry Hill Dr, Conroe, TX 77303. We handle bookkeeping cleanup, catch-up, monthly bookkeeping, reconciliations, and QuickBooks ProAdvisor management of QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Desktop for small businesses, nonprofits, and churches across Texas. We are not a CPA firm and do not provide tax filing or audit services — we work alongside your CPA.

Are you a CPA firm?

No. WestgateFS is a bookkeeping and operational accounting practice. We do not provide tax preparation, audit, attest, or other CPA-regulated services. We work alongside your CPA, providing the clean books and operational reporting that make their work faster and more accurate.

Where is WestgateFS located?

We are based at 15834 Berry Hill Dr, Conroe, TX 77303 and serve Texas businesses statewide, with anchored expertise in Houston, Dallas, and Austin. Our work is fully remote-capable on QuickBooks Online and QuickBooks Desktop.

How is monthly bookkeeping priced?

Monthly bookkeeping is priced based on transaction volume, the number of bank and credit card accounts, the complexity of your industry, and the reporting cadence you need. Most Texas small businesses fall into honest, predictable monthly ranges. We never quote until we’ve reviewed a recent month of activity. See our pricing approach.

How long does bookkeeping cleanup take?

Most bookkeeping cleanups take between four and ten weeks, depending on how many months are out of order, how many accounts need reconciliation, and how clean the source documents are. We give a clear timeline after a free diagnostic review of your QuickBooks file. More on cleanup.

Do you work with churches and nonprofits?

Yes. WestgateFS has long-standing experience serving churches, faith-based ministries, and small nonprofits across Texas. We provide fund-style tracking, designated and restricted-giving handling, and board-ready monthly reporting. We do not prepare Form 990 returns — we partner with your CPA for that. Nonprofit bookkeeping · Church bookkeeping.

What’s the difference between WestgateFS and QuickBooks Live?

QuickBooks Live is Intuit’s productized bookkeeping service, staffed by rotating bookkeepers. WestgateFS is an independent QuickBooks ProAdvisor practice run by senior operators. You work with the same operators every month, with full responsibility for your books — not a help-desk queue. See the comparison.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. We use encrypted file sharing, least-privilege access controls, multi-factor authentication on every system, and secure QuickBooks accountant access — never shared admin logins. Read our data security practices.
